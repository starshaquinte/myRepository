The vibrant hues of the sunset painted the sky in a spectacular arry of colors. The fiery reds and oranges gradually gave way to softer pinks and purples as the sun dipped below the horizon. The wispy clouds caught the light and appeared to be fire, casting long shadows across the landscape. The trees and fields below were bathed in a warm golden glow.

The glow faded to blue as the darkness of night crept in.The last remnants of light disappeared.The stars emerged like tiny pinpricks in the deep blue canvas.
